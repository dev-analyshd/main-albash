## üîë Key Concepts Documented

### Three Core Rules of the Bridge

1. **One Asset, One State**
   - Asset exists in Web2 OR Web3, never both simultaneously
   - Bridging locks one side, activates the other

2. **Value Parity**
   - $100 in Web2 = $100 in Web3 (USDC)
   - No inflation, no speculation premiums
   - Value perfectly preserved

3. **Verification is Global**
   - Verify once ‚Üí access everywhere
   - Reputation travels with asset
   - Trust is portable

### Three Operational Modes

1. **Web2 Mode** (Traditional users)
   - Fiat payments (NGN, USD)
   - Email/Google/X login
   - Digital marketplace

2. **Web3 Mode** (Crypto-native users)
   - Stablecoin payments
   - Wallet login
   - Smart contract interactions

3. **Full Mode** (Institutions)
   - Mixed crypto + non-crypto users
   - Multi-currency support
   - Bridge operations
